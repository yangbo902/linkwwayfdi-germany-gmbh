ABOUT KUHNPOHL

OUR STORY

FORM FOLLOWS NATURE

Founded in Zurich in 2010, Kuhnpohl Luxury Glamping Resort emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create outdoor accommodations that don't conquer nature, but harmonize with it.

Our founders, Klaus Müller and Sofia Bergström, trained in German Bauhaus principles and Swiss precision engineering, saw an opportunity to revolutionize the outdoor hospitality industry.

PHILOSOPHY

We believe luxury should enhance nature, not dominate it. Every structure we design follows the principles of environmental harmony, sustainable craftsmanship, and timeless aesthetics.

Today, we've completed over 50 projects across 12 European countries, each one a testament to our unwavering commitment to sustainability, craftsmanship, and design excellence.

From the Swiss Alps to the Italian coastline, our resorts blend seamlessly with their natural surroundings while offering uncompromising luxury and comfort.

RECOGNITION

Our work has been recognized with 15 international design awards, including the prestigious European Design Award and the German Sustainability Prize. We've been featured in Architectural Digest, Wallpaper*, and Dezeen.

OUR DESIGN PROCESS

FROM CONCEPT TO REALITY

Each Kuhnpohl structure undergoes a rigorous 8-stage development process, from initial site analysis through final certification. Our team of architects, engineers, and craftsmen collaborate to ensure every detail meets our exacting standards.

STAGE 1-4: DESIGN

Site analysis, conceptual design, architectural development, and engineering specifications. We conduct environmental impact assessments and work with local authorities to ensure compliance.

STAGE 5-8: EXECUTION

Manufacturing at our Swiss facility, quality control testing, transportation and site preparation, final assembly and commissioning. Every structure is tested in climate chambers simulating extreme conditions.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

All products carry CE certification, achieve Passive House standards, and come with 25-year structural warranty. We conduct annual inspections for the first 5 years.

GENERAL QUESTIONS
WHAT IS KUHNPOHL?
+
Kuhnpohl is Europe's premier luxury glamping brand, specializing in the design, construction, and operation of high-end outdoor accommodations. Founded in Zurich in 2010, we operate 50+ resorts across 12 European countries, combining German engineering precision with natural aesthetics.
WHERE ARE YOUR RESORTS LOCATED?
+
Our resorts span across Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Italy, France, Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Netherlands, Belgium, Czech Republic, and Poland. Locations range from alpine mountains and dense forests to lakesides and coastal areas.
WHAT MAKES KUHNPOHL DIFFERENT?
+
Our unique approach combines Bauhaus design principles with sustainable construction methods. Every structure achieves LEED Platinum certification, features smart technology, and is custom-designed to harmonize with its natural environment. We don't just build accommodations—we create immersive nature experiences with zero compromise on luxury.
BOOKING & RESERVATIONS
HOW DO I MAKE A RESERVATION?
+
Reservations can be made through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our concierge team directly. Kuhnpohl Club members receive priority booking access and can reserve stays up to 12 months in advance. Non-members can book up to 6 months ahead.
WHAT IS YOUR CANCELLATION POLICY?
+
Cancellations made 30+ days before arrival receive full refund. 14-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 14 days: no refund unless rebooking is successful. Platinum members enjoy flexible cancellation up to 7 days before arrival with full refund.
WHAT ARE YOUR RATES?
+
Rates vary by resort, season, and accommodation type. Standard stays range from €450-€1,200 per night. Premium Gipfel Suites start at €1,800 per night. All stays include breakfast, wifi, parking, and access to resort amenities. Kuhnpohl Club members receive 10-30% discount depending on tier.
MEMBERSHIP
WHAT ARE THE MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS?
+
Members receive priority booking, substantial discounts (10-30%), complimentary upgrades, access to exclusive events, personal concierge service, and guest passes. Higher tiers include airport transfers, private chef service, and guaranteed availability even during peak seasons.
CAN I UPGRADE MY MEMBERSHIP?
+
Yes, members can upgrade at any time. The price difference will be prorated based on the remaining months in your membership year. Contact our membership team for details.
SUSTAINABILITY
HOW SUSTAINABLE ARE YOUR RESORTS?
+
All Kuhnpohl resorts achieve LEED Platinum certification. We use 100% renewable energy, harvest rainwater, treat wastewater naturally, and source materials locally. Our structures are designed for minimal site impact with reversible foundations. We've achieved net-zero carbon operations across all properties since 2020.
WHAT IS YOUR ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY?
+
We follow strict environmental protocols including zero single-use plastics, organic linens and toiletries, electric vehicle charging at all locations, composting programs, and partnerships with local conservation organizations. We conduct annual environmental audits and publish sustainability reports.
PRODUCTS & PURCHASING

WHAT IS THE DELIVERY TIMELINE?

Standard products: 6-8 months from order confirmation. Custom designs: 10-14 months. This includes manufacturing, transportation, foundation work, and full installation. We handle all permitting and coordination with local contractors.
